Block Performance Dashboard

This dashboard is Amrita's primary reference for business performance — a single source of truth covering where we are pacing, what's changed, and what needs attention. Updated each Tuesday by Corporate FP&A, it consolidates quarterly projections and year-over-year trends into one view for innercore.

What's Inside

Quarterly Pacing

The current quarter's performance snapshot, refreshed weekly. Includes:

  • Current quarter projections with week-over-week movement
  • Year-over-year growth performance across Block, Cash App, and Square
  • Performance versus plan and guidance for each financial and operational KPI
  • Key takeaways summarized by Corporate FP&A

Additional Updates

Material watch points and commentary from Block's strategic finance partners on business performance trends, emerging risks, and any items requiring executive attention.

FY2026 Outlook Preliminary — Under Development

A forward-looking view of the full-year forecast — tracking Block's growth and profitability outlook relative to external consensus estimates throughout the year. This section is actively being built out.

Discussion & Comments

Each page includes a Discussion section at the bottom where team members can post comments and tag colleagues using @name (e.g., @amrita). Tagged individuals receive an email notification with the comment and a link to the dashboard.

An automated refresh runs at 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM PT each Tuesday to ensure all comments are visible to everyone.

Please do not use the "Clear all" button — Corporate FP&A will clear comment threads as needed.
