NM

Were there any quarters where we missed our operating income guidance, and why? What did we say externally vs. what was the actual driver internally?

AI

Yes — Q3 2024 Adjusted Operating Income came in at $537M, below our internal guidance range of $560–580M. Here's what we communicated externally vs. the internal context:

◆ External Narrative

"Adjusted operating income of $537M reflected ongoing investment in our AI and machine learning capabilities, as well as the impact of seasonal hiring patterns. We remain confident in our full-year profitability outlook."

"We continue to expect meaningful operating leverage in the back half of 2024."

Source: Q3 2024 Shareholder Letter • Q3 2024 Earnings Call Transcript
◆ Internal Context

AOI miss was primarily driven by $28M in unplanned Cash App risk losses related to the September fraud spike, plus $15M in accelerated platform infrastructure spend that was pulled forward from Q4.

The hiring narrative was directionally accurate but was not the primary driver. Risk losses were the surprise.

Source: Q3 2024 Board Pre-Read • October 2024 Monthly Reporting Pack
NM

How has our narrative on Square GPV growth evolved over the past year?

AI

The Square GPV narrative has shifted notably across 2025:

◆ External Narrative

Q1 2025: "We see encouraging signs of stabilization in US GPV growth, particularly in our restaurant and retail verticals."

Q2 2025: "International expansion continues to be a bright spot, with markets like Japan and France driving double-digit growth."

Q4 2025: "Square GPV is benefiting from our go-upmarket strategy, with mid-market sellers growing 23% YoY."

Source: Q1–Q4 2025 Shareholder Letters
◆ Internal Context

Q1 2025: US GPV growth was decelerating faster than forecast. The stabilization messaging was anchored to one month of data (March).

Q2 2025: International growth was real but partially offset by FX headwinds. Constant-currency growth was +19% vs. reported +16%.

Q4 2025: Mid-market traction was genuine. Board discussion focused on whether it was sustainable or driven by onboarding incentives.

Source: Monthly Reporting Packs • Board Pre-Reads • Weekly Digests
